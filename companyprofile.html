About Razorpay

Razorpay is India's leading full-stack financial solutions company. We offer cutting-edge payment solutions and banking products that help businesses accept, process, and disburse payments with ease. Trusted by over 8 million businesses, Razorpay is redefining financial infrastructure for the digital era.

Our engineering team works on complex distributed systems, real-time payment processing, fraud detection ML models, and developer-first APIs that power millions of transactions every day.
